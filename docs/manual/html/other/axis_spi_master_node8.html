Ports

A port list is availabe, with specific signal information, in the . The generalized idea is a AXIS slave input for MOSI data. A AXIS master output for MISO data, and a master SPI interface with sclk, mosi, miso, and ss_n signals. The dcount outputs give insight into the status of the in/out data bits of the core.
